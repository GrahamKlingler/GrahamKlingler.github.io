Ghost Recon

Tricks

Here are a few tricks: 1.If you need to go down in your helicopter fast to escape a missile or a SAM launcher, you can switch seats. 2.If you are chasing a car and you are about one hundred meters behind, stop suddenly and fly out the windshield and end up in front of the car.

Tips

Here are a few tips: 1.To do more damage, take off your suppressor. 2.Explosive drones can be great for assassination missions.
